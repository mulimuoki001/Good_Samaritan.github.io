First Donation By Marriot Hotel Rwanda

The Good Samaritan Project reached a milestone with Marriott Kigali Hotel emerging as the trailblazer, making the inaugural food donation in support of the initiative. This significant contribution by Marriott Kigali sets a commendable standard, exemplifying the hotel's commitment to corporate social responsibility and community engagement. By taking a proactive stance in addressing issues of hunger and food waste, Marriott Kigali Hotel not only fulfills a critical role in the Good Samaritan Project but also becomes a pioneering force in the hospitality sector in Rwanda. This gesture underscores the potential for businesses to be catalysts for positive change, leveragingtheir resources to benefit the broader community.


Good Samaritan Project represents a noteworthy collaboration between the hospitality industry and social welfare initiatives in Rwanda. The decision to make the first food donation showcases the hotel's dedication to local community welfare and aligns with the broader goals of the initiative. Beyond being a generous contributor, Marriott Kigali's involvement serves as an inspiration for other hotels and businesses in the region, encouraging them to recognize their capacity to address social and environmental challenges. This proactive engagement by Marriott Kigali marks not just a singular act of goodwill but the initiation of a potential industry-wide movement toward responsible and impactful corporate citizenship.


The impact of Marriott Kigali Hotel's pioneering role extends beyond the immediate donation, contributing to the overarching objectives of the Good Samaritan Project. As the project gains momentum, Marriott Kigali sets a precedent for other stakeholders, illustrating the transformative potential of collaborative efforts between businesses and social initiatives. The hotel's leadership in this initiative not only addresses critical issues of food waste and hunger but also positions Marriott Kigali as a beacon of corporate responsibility, showcasing the positive influence that businesses can wield in creating a more compassionate and sustainable society in Rwanda.
